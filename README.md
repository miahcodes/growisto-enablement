## Key Themes

- **AI-first commerce**: Shopify's AI is embedded across the platform — search, content, personalization, automation, and analytics
- **India enterprise focus**: Multilingual content, festive season strategies, regional personalization, Tier 2/3 city considerations
- **Speed to value**: Most AI features activate in hours/days, not months — a major advantage over Salesforce/Adobe alternatives
- **Compounding returns**: AI features improve with more data; month 3+ results exceed month 1 by 3-5x

Growisto is a Shopify Plus partner specializing in enabling AI-powered commerce for India's enterprise merchants. We help brands leverage Shopify's full AI stack to drive conversion, reduce operational costs, and compete with marketplace giants.
